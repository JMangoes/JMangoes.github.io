El Tráfico

On August 23rd, LAFC will face off against their biggest rival, the LA Galaxy, as this rivalry is also known as, El Tráfico.

Not only do we want LAFC to win against the Galaxy, but we also want to show that we have the best fan base! LAFC fans will get the opportnity to showcase their loud and fun spirit through an event called, Dignity Take Over, where we will invade the home of the Galaxy.

To make this rivalry even more interesting, LAFC and LA Galaxy have agreed to have a trophy raise to whomever wins El Tráfico. And fans can be a part of history through an El Tráfico trophy drawing contest.
