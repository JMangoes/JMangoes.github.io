Dignity Take Over

Not only is LAFC the better LA soccer team, but we also have the best fans. To show how strong the fanbase is, LAFC will host a pre-game rally at Victoria Park, located in Carson, approximately one mile away from the home of the LA Galaxy.

The rally will begin at 10am and is open to all fans, as there will be food, drinks, and a live DJ. Key LAFC figures wil also be in attendance such as Tom Penn, Will Ferrel, and Magic Johnson. At 1pm, our key figures will lead a march to Dignity Health Sports Park as all fans are welcome to march, with a ticket or no ticket to the game.
